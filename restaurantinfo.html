Yum Yum's

About us: Are you looking for great flavour without breaking the bank? Then come to Yum Yum's! Here at Yum Yum's, we serve the finest Chinese food on the University of Toronto campus, all while keeping the prices affordable. Are you a UofT student? We offer various discounts to students!<

Unlike a typical Western restaurant, Yum Yum's serves dishes to the table, rather than the individual, meaning that you can share and try as much food as you like! We even offer take-out. Just let us know what and roughly when you want your food, and it will be ready for you!
