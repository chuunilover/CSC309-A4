Frequently Asked Questions


What is Restaurant Reserver?
Restaurant Reserver is a convenient web application which allows you to view information of and reserve seats for many restaurants! This allows you to choose a seat at the time of your choosing all from the convenience of your own home, without all the lengthy waiting required to have staff check and confirm your reservation.
How do I begin using Restaurant Reserver?
First of all, set up your account or log in. Then use the top bar to search for a restaurant by name or cuisine. Then choose the restaurant you want, choose the time you want from that restaurant and click a non-red seat to reserve that seat. It's that simple.
Is there a time limit on my sitting?
This will depend on the restaurant you go to. Most offer a 90 minute seating time limit.
